# -Put in Pressure Cooker - 

	3-6lbs of Live Whole Lobster
	2.5 cups water
	2 Tbsp Kosher Salt


# -Set pressure Cooker - 
	
	4mins Pressure Cook - Manual High pressure setting
	Quick Pressure Release


# -Serve hot with butter-
	
	Butter heated and clarified 


Enjoy!
